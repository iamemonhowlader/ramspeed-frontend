Our mission

At RAMSPEED CY , our mission is to provide complete technology solutions that meet every customer's need. We specialize in the repair and sale of mobile phones, computers, and tablets, as well as printers and both original and compatible printing supplies, such as inks, toners, rolls, printing paper, and more. In addition, we offer mobile accessories, security cameras, networking systems, and solar installation solutions — covering needs in energy efficiency, security, lighting, and connectivity. Our goal is to deliver reliability, quality, and prompt service by offering products and services that make a real difference in our customers' daily lives.

Our vision

Our aim is to remain a trusted and recognizable name in Cyprus in the field of technology. Our vision is continuous growth and innovation, providing modern, sustainable, and affordable technology solutions that cover every area — from mobile and computer repairs to security systems, networking infrastructures, and photovoltaic installations. We believe in the power of technology to make life easier, safer, and more connected.

Our story

RAMSPEED CY has its roots in Onoufrios Enterprises LTD, founded by Mr. Onoufrios Onoufriou in 2003. In its early years, the company specialized in printers and printing supplies such as computers, inks, toners, printing papers , rolls, and security systems. Over the years, the business evolved and expanded its scope, investing in new technologies, networking systems, and related services. Today, RAMSPEED CY stands as a complete technology center, offering repairs and sales of mobile phones, computers, tablets, security cameras, solar solutions, and a wide range of tech products that meet every modern need.
